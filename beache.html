THE LAND OF SERENDIPITY

Mirissa Beach

Mirissa beach is located between the major cities of gale and Matara. This is a pretty busy beach because it’s very popular amount tourists.it is a shiny golden beach surrounded by palm trees. Those palm trees are so special if you google images of Sri Lanka the well-known palm tree photo will be the fifth picture you see.

Mirissa beach is the best place to relax with your loved ones. There are also more than 60 restaurants with delicious foods as your wish and also beach clubs and coffee shops . also there’s a secret beach in mirissa beach it is a small secluded beach tucked around the corner from the main beach in mirissa and its little harder to get to.

Hiriketiya Beach

Hiriketiya beach is such an amazing calm place to spend your evening it’s a very lovely view, this is one of the south coast beaches in Sri Lanka The south coast is one of Sri Lanka’s most rewarding areas to visit. the main attraction of this beach is surfing.

There are lots of amazing things to do on hiriketiya beach like surfing, hanging out at verse collectives, swinging at mahi-mahi, going to dikwella beach in town, playing clubbing games, and also you can spend your day in Tangalle, Tangalle is just 25 minutes from dikwella

Uppuveli Beach

Uppuweli is the main beach in the center of Trincomalee. It’s a lovely long beach .it is a beautiful sandy beach with amazing palms and a wide coastline. Calm waves and beautiful colors on the beach are the main things about uppuveli beach.

A lot of hotels and restaurants are located along this beach which is why most tourists stay within walking distance . uppuweli is a more popular beach destination with fancy beach bars. You can also do whale watching in Trincomalee the season starts in May and ends in September
